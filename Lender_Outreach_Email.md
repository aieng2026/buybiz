# Lender Outreach — SBA PLP Pre-Qualification

> Goal: get pre-qualified with 2–3 SBA **Preferred Lenders (PLP)**. Reach out before you have a target.
> Pick lenders active in your target verticals (cleaning, MSP, landscaping, self-storage, RV/boat storage, car wash).

---

## VERSION 1 — Cold email to a PLP lender

**Subject:** SBA 7(a) acquisition pre-qual — citizen buyer, $600K+ EBITDA target

Hi [Lender name],

I'm an individual buyer preparing to acquire and operate one established business, and I'm looking to get pre-qualified with an SBA Preferred Lender before I go under LOI.

**My profile:**
- **U.S. citizen**, [credit score band, e.g., 720+]
- **Equity available:** [$___] liquid for the injection, plus reserves
- **Target deal size:** $2.4–9M (EBITDA $600K+), open to a seller note on full standby
- **Industries:** commercial cleaning, IT managed services, commercial landscaping, self-storage, RV/boat storage, car wash

- **Background:** career operator across multiple industries (agriculture, equipment rental/ops, hospitality, technical education, multi-state distribution, print services) — buying to operate and grow

Could we set up a call to review my profile for pre-qualification, and discuss your appetite and typical terms for acquisitions in these verticals? I'd also value your read on structuring the equity injection (cash vs. seller note on standby).

## Questions to ask each lender on the call
1. Are you a **PLP** (can you approve in-house)? What's your current approval timeline?
2. What's your **appetite** for [industry] acquisitions in [state]?
3. How do you treat the **equity injection** — minimum cash vs. seller note on standby?
4. **DSCR** you underwrite to (1.25x floor, or do you want 1.5x)?
5. Do you require a **QoE**? At what deal size?
6. Typical **rate, term, fees** for an acquisition my size?
7. What kills deals for you in underwriting?

## Tips
- Contact **2–3 PLP lenders** and compare — appetite and speed vary widely.
- Have your **personal document packet** ready (see SBA_PreQual_Checklist.md).
- Fill the bracketed fields before sending; lenders respond to specifics.
- A pre-qual letter in hand is the credibility unlock for your broker outreach.
